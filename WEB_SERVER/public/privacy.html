1. Overview

We respect player privacy and only process information that is needed to operate the game, provide online features, protect fair play, improve stability, respond to support requests, and comply with legal or platform requirements.

This policy covers personal information, device information, gameplay data, support information, and data processed by trusted service providers used by the game.

2. Data We Collect

The exact information collected depends on the features you use, your device settings, your region, and the platform services enabled for your account.

Account and profile data Player ID, display name, avatar or profile image if provided, Google Play Games or Firebase authentication identifiers, friend code, account status, and login records.
Gameplay and online match data Match history, room information, ranking, score, inventory, virtual items, achievements, rewards, anti-cheat events, connection state, and server logs needed to run multiplayer sessions.
Device and diagnostics data Device model, operating system version, app version, language, network connection information, IP address, approximate region, crash reports, performance logs, and error diagnostics.
Ads, rewards, and purchases Advertising identifiers where allowed, ad interaction data, rewarded ad completion status, fraud prevention signals, and Google Play purchase status. We do not receive full payment card numbers from Google Play.
Support and communication data Email address and message content when you contact support, request account deletion, report a problem, or send feedback.
Optional user content Information you choose to submit through social, friend, profile, market, or community features made available in the game.

3. How We Use Data

  • To create, authenticate, and maintain player accounts.
  • To run online matches, rooms, rankings, rewards, inventory, and game progress.
  • To provide support, troubleshoot bugs, improve performance, and recover accounts.
  • To detect abuse, cheating, fraud, spam, security incidents, and policy violations.
  • To serve ads or rewarded ads when enabled, measure ad performance, and prevent ad fraud.
  • To process purchase entitlements and virtual item delivery through Google Play.
  • To comply with law, platform policies, safety requirements, and lawful requests.

4. When We Share Data

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with service providers and platforms that help us operate the game. These providers process data under their own privacy terms and technical safeguards.

  • Authentication and platform services, such as Google Play Games Services and Firebase.
  • Hosting, database, server monitoring, and multiplayer infrastructure providers.
  • Analytics, crash reporting, diagnostics, and performance monitoring providers.
  • Advertising and rewarded ad providers, such as Google AdMob or Google Mobile Ads, when ads are enabled.
  • Payment processors, including Google Play Billing, for purchase confirmation and fraud prevention.
  • Law enforcement, regulators, or other parties when required to protect users, rights, safety, or legal obligations.

5. Device Permissions and Sensitive Data

The game requests only the permissions needed for gameplay, platform services, security, networking, ads, and optional features. Android may show permissions used by included SDKs even when a specific feature is optional or only active in certain builds.

  • Internet access is used for login, multiplayer, rewards, support, updates, ads, and server communication.
  • Vibration may be used for gameplay feedback.
  • Bluetooth, Wi-Fi state, or approximate location may be requested by Google Play Games or nearby networking features where required by Android. We do not use precise GPS location for core gameplay.
  • We do not request SMS, call log, contacts, camera, microphone, or precise background location for core gameplay.

If a future feature requires a new sensitive permission, we will explain the purpose before the permission is used and update this policy when required.

6. Children and Families

Ban Bi Tuoi Tho may appeal to a broad audience, including younger players. If a player is under the age required by local law to manage their own account or give valid consent, the player should use the game only with permission from a parent or legal guardian.

We do not knowingly collect more personal information from children than is reasonably necessary to provide the game. If we learn that we collected personal information from a child in a way that does not meet applicable requirements, we will take reasonable steps to delete or limit that information.

  • Parents or guardians may contact us to review, correct, or request deletion of a child's information.
  • If ads are served to children or users of unknown age, they should be served only through ad providers allowed for child-directed services where required by Google Play policy.
  • We encourage parents to supervise online play, friend features, purchases, and ad interactions.

7. Data Retention

We keep information only for as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy, including gameplay continuity, account recovery, fraud prevention, dispute handling, security, and legal requirements.

  • Account, inventory, ranking, and match data may be retained while your account is active.
  • Server logs, anti-cheat logs, diagnostic records, and security records may be retained for a limited period unless needed for investigation or compliance.
  • Support messages may be retained to resolve your request and maintain a support history.
  • Deleted account data is removed or anonymized within a reasonable period unless retention is required for security, fraud prevention, legal, tax, or dispute reasons.

8. Account and Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your game account and associated personal data by emailing us from the account email, or by providing enough information for us to verify ownership of the player account.

Account deletion request

Email ringthe60@gmail.com with the subject "Ban Bi Tuoi Tho account deletion request". Include your player ID, friend code, login method, and any details that help us identify the account.

Some information may be retained after deletion if necessary for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, payment records, or unresolved disputes. When retention is not required, we will delete or anonymize the data.

9. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information, including access controls, server-side authentication, secure transport where available, monitoring, and restricted access to operational systems.

No online service is completely secure. Players should keep their platform accounts safe, avoid sharing login credentials, and report suspicious activity to us.

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, export, restrict, or object to certain processing of your personal information. You may also control some data through your device settings, Google Play settings, ad personalization settings, or platform account settings.

  • You can manage Google Play Games and Google account settings through Google services.
  • You can contact us for privacy requests, account deletion, or questions about data handling.
